FAQs

1. How long does it take to process a visa?

Visa processing typically takes 2–5 business days depending on the destination and applicant’s nationality.

2. What documents are needed?

A valid passport, invitation letter (if any), passport photo, and travel itinerary. Additional documents may be requested based on the country.

3. Can I book a visa without booking a flight?

Yes, we offer standalone visa processing as well as flight + visa packages.

4. Do you provide visa support for all countries?

We provide support for most countries across Europe, North America, Asia, and Africa. Contact us to confirm for specific countries.
